I'm a PhD stuent in SUSTech, China. My advisor is Professor Yifei Zhu. My reseach interests are algebraic topology, algebraic geometry, number theory and theoritical physics. You can find my CV here， and Research Statement here.

Email: xuecai.u.ma@foxmial.com

Currently, I focus on following problems.

  • Derive algebraic geometry methods in algebriac topology and number theory, especially derived moduli sapces, dervied-rings.

  • Derived rings and p-adic cohomology theories, like topological Hochschild homology, prismatic cohomology.

  • Stolz-Teichner program, which gives an relation between quantum field theories and generalized cohomology theories.
